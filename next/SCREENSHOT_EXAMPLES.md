## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Better Screenshots

### **Before You Capture:**

1. **Clean Your Browser:**
   - Hide bookmarks bar (Cmd+Shift+B on Mac, Ctrl+Shift+B on Windows)
   - Close unnecessary tabs
   - Remove browser extensions from toolbar
   - Use incognito/private mode if needed

2. **Check Your Content:**
   - No lorem ipsum text
   - Real images (not placeholder.com)
   - No "Coming Soon" pages
   - Working links and buttons
   - Spell-check everything visible

3. **Optimize Window Size:**
   - F12 â†’ Toggle Device Toolbar
   - Set to specific dimensions (1920x1080)
   - Or just maximize browser window
   - Remove zoom (set to 100%)

### **While Recording GIFs:**

1. **Move Slowly:**
   - Scroll at 50% your normal speed
   - Pause for 1-2 seconds on important elements
   - Click deliberately (not rapid-fire)

2. **Show Intent:**
   - Hover before clicking (shows interactivity)
   - Let animations complete before moving on
   - Demonstrate one feature at a time

3. **Plan Your Path:**
   - Know exactly what you'll click before recording
   - Practice once without recording
   - 3 takes max (keep it fresh, not rehearsed)

### **After Capturing:**

1. **Review Quality:**
   - Is text readable?
   - Are colors accurate?
   - Is the main subject clear?
   - Any UI glitches visible?

2. **Optimize Files:**
   - Compress images with TinyPNG.com
   - Compress GIFs with Ezgif.com
   - Keep originals as backup
   - Check file sizes before sending

3. **Organize Files:**
   - Use descriptive filenames
   - Group by project
   - Include a version number if iterating
